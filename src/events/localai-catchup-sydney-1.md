Our first Sydney catchup, running on the side of the WCUC conference at ICC Sydney. Whether you're experimenting with models on your own machine, exploring new tools, or simply curious about where local AI is heading, this is a good chance to hear what others are working on and share your own perspective. Hosted by Alexar, the session will be relaxed and focused on useful conversation rather than formal presentations.

**Discussion Topics**
- Claude + Obsidian "second brain" workflows, and Andrej Karpathy's llm wiki approach ([karpathy.ai](https://karpathy.ai), [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/artificialintelligence-innovation-technology-ugcPost-7475543261869432832-3kRP/), [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/doshkim_ive-gone-through-almost-every-wiki-style-ugcPost-7431293613516521472-EVYk/))
- Fine-tuning on your own hardware with [QLoRA](https://arxiv.org/abs/2305.14314) and [Unsloth](https://unsloth.ai)
- Local/distributed AI vs data-centre business models, prompted by [Thinking Machines' Inkling](https://thinkingmachines.ai/news/introducing-inkling/)
- An AI-designed Linux kernel? [Linus Torvalds shared this](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/overnight-linux-creator-linus-torvalds-share-7483442692589494272-CJlk/)
- Huddle App, a note-taking app powered by Ollama
- Our upcoming [Building a Second Brain w/ Obsidian](/events/building-an-obsidian-brain/) meetup in Melbourne
- 🤩 BYO Topic!
